About LetsTalk

Where everyone's voice matters equally

Our Mission

LetsTalk is built on a simple but powerful idea: equal engagement. On traditional social media, some voices get amplified while others go unheard. We believe everyone deserves to be heard equally.

How It Works

1

Listen First

To create a post, you must first respond to someone else's post. This ensures you're actively listening and engaging with your community.

2

Earn Credits

Each meaningful response earns you 1 credit (max 3). Spend 1 credit to create your own post. This creates a balanced give-and-take.

3

Quality Over Quantity

With a 3 post per day limit, we encourage thoughtful posts over endless scrolling. Your words matter here.

4

Relevant Responses

Our validation system ensures responses are relevant to the original post, promoting genuine conversation over spam.

Why Equal Engagement?

Traditional social media creates inequality:

  • Popular users get overwhelming attention
  • Many voices go completely unheard
  • Algorithms favor engagement over quality
  • Passive consumption outweighs active participation

LetsTalk flips this model by requiring participation before posting. Everyone must contribute to the conversation before adding to it.
